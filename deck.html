Workshop Demo

Marketing Momentum

A sample workshop deck designed to show clear facilitation flow, thoughtful reflection, and polished participant-facing structure.

Focus: helping participants build visibility through clarity, consistency, and intentional action.

Slide 1 of 6
Session Objectives

What participants should leave with

  • Clearer marketing priorities
  • One high-leverage channel to focus on
  • A practical next-step action plan
Slide 2 of 6
Key Principle

Most brands don’t lack ideas. They lack a system for showing up consistently.

Small repeated actions build more momentum than occasional bursts of effort.

Slide 3 of 6
Participant Exercise

Choose your primary channel

Identify the one platform or channel that deserves your focus for the next 90 days.

Ask yourself

Where does your audience already pay attention?

Then decide

Which channel will receive your most consistent effort?

Slide 4 of 6
Reflection Prompt

What has been getting in the way?

Reflect on the habits, assumptions, or distractions that have prevented consistency in your marketing.

Journal prompt: What pattern do I need to interrupt in order to build momentum?

Slide 5 of 6
Action Step

Turn insight into movement

What is one action you will take in the next 48 hours to move your marketing forward?

Takeaway: clarity plus consistency creates momentum.
